# Takeaway Policy

## Takeaway availability

Takeaway is available only when the live ordering system presents it as an available fulfilment option.

The customer must select the intended collection branch before confirming the order.

The ordering assistant must not promise takeaway from a branch that is unavailable or not shown by the live ordering system.

## Estimated preparation time

The typical takeaway preparation estimate for the demonstration restaurant is 20 to 30 minutes after order confirmation.

This is an estimate, not a guarantee.

Preparation time may change because of:

* Order size
* Item preparation requirements
* Restaurant order volume
* Temporary equipment or staffing issues
* Operational disruption

The customer should rely on the latest order information returned by the live ordering system.

## Order confirmation

A takeaway order is confirmed only when the ordering system explicitly confirms the order and provides an order reference or order ID.

A cart, draft order or pending checkout is not a confirmed takeaway order.

## Collection location

The customer must collect the order from the branch selected during checkout.

The ordering assistant must not redirect the customer to a different branch unless the live system or restaurant support confirms the change.

Branch address and contact information are maintained in the approved branch documents.

## Collecting the order

The customer should provide the order reference when collecting the order.

The restaurant may also request the customer name, contact detail or other reasonable information associated with the order to prevent collection by an unauthorized person.

Sensitive account, payment or authentication information should not be requested for routine order collection.

## Early arrival

Customers who arrive before the order is ready may be asked to wait.

The restaurant cannot guarantee that preparation will be accelerated because the customer arrives early.

The ordering assistant must not state that an order is ready unless the live ordering system confirms its readiness.

## Late collection

Customers should collect takeaway orders as close as reasonably possible to the estimated ready time.

Food temperature, texture and overall quality may decline when an order is collected late.

The restaurant does not guarantee reheating, replacement or remaking of an order because the customer arrived late.

## Holding uncollected orders

The restaurant may hold a prepared takeaway order for up to 30 minutes after its estimated ready time.

After that period, the restaurant may discard the order when necessary for food quality, food safety or operational reasons.

The restaurant is not required to keep prepared food indefinitely.

A refund, remake or account credit for an uncollected order is not automatic and must be reviewed under the approved refund policy.

## Collection by another person

Another person may collect the order when the customer authorizes them and provides them with the correct order reference.

The restaurant may refuse collection when the collector cannot provide sufficient order information or when there is a reasonable concern about unauthorized collection.

The restaurant should not disclose unnecessary customer information to the collector.

## Changes after confirmation

Customers may change cart contents before confirming the order.

After confirmation, changes are not guaranteed because preparation may already have started.

The customer should contact restaurant support as soon as possible. The ordering assistant must not confirm a change unless the live system or restaurant support verifies it.

## Cancellation

A takeaway order may be cancelled only according to the approved refund and cancellation policy.

Cancellation is not guaranteed after preparation has started.

The ordering assistant must not promise a refund or successful cancellation without confirmation from the appropriate system or restaurant support.

## Missing or incorrect items

Customers should inspect the order within a reasonable time after collection.

Missing, incorrect or damaged items should be reported through the ordering assistant or restaurant support with the order reference and a description of the issue.

A replacement, refund or account credit is not automatic and must follow the approved complaint and refund policies.

## Unsupported takeaway requests

The ordering assistant must not promise:

* An exact guaranteed ready time
* Immediate preparation
* Collection from a different branch without confirmation
* Indefinite order holding
* Automatic remaking of a late-collected order
* Automatic refunds for uncollected orders
* Curbside collection unless shown by the live ordering system
* That an order is ready without live system confirmation
