# Takeaway Policy

## Takeaway availability

Takeaway is available only when the live ordering system presents it as an available fulfilment option.

Customers must select the intended collection branch before confirming the order.

Takeaway cannot be guaranteed from a branch that is unavailable or not displayed by the ordering system.

## Estimated preparation time

The typical preparation estimate for a takeaway order is 20 to 30 minutes after order confirmation.

This is an estimate and not a guaranteed ready time.

Preparation time may change because of:

* Order size
* Item preparation requirements
* Restaurant order volume
* Temporary equipment or staffing issues
* Operational disruptions

Customers should rely on the latest preparation and order-status information provided by the live ordering system.

## Order confirmation

A takeaway order is confirmed only when the ordering system explicitly confirms it and provides an order reference or order ID.

A cart, draft order or pending checkout is not a confirmed takeaway order.

## Collection location

Customers must collect the order from the branch selected during checkout.

Collection from another branch requires confirmation from the live ordering system or restaurant staff.

Customers should confirm the displayed branch and collection address before completing the order.

## Collecting the order

Customers should provide the order reference when collecting the order.

The restaurant may also request reasonable information associated with the order, such as the customer name or contact detail, to prevent unauthorized collection.

Customers should not provide passwords, banking credentials, card information, one-time passwords or private authentication codes during routine collection.

## Order readiness

An order should be treated as ready for collection only when the live ordering system or restaurant staff confirms that it is ready.

An estimated preparation time does not confirm readiness.

## Early arrival

Customers arriving before the order is ready may be asked to wait.

The restaurant cannot guarantee that preparation will be accelerated because a customer arrives early.

## Late collection

Customers should collect takeaway orders as close as reasonably possible to the estimated ready time.

Food temperature, texture and overall quality may decline when collection is delayed.

The restaurant does not guarantee reheating, replacement or remaking because the customer arrived late.

## Holding uncollected orders

The restaurant may hold a prepared takeaway order for up to 30 minutes after its estimated ready time.

After that period, the restaurant may dispose of the order when necessary for food quality, food safety or operational reasons.

The restaurant is not required to keep prepared food indefinitely.

A refund, remake or account credit for an uncollected order is not automatic and requires review under the refund and cancellation policy.

## Collection by another person

Another person may collect the order when the customer authorizes them and provides them with the correct order reference.

The restaurant may refuse collection when the person cannot provide sufficient order information or when there is a reasonable concern about unauthorized collection.

Unnecessary customer information will not be disclosed to the person collecting the order.

## Changes after confirmation

Customers may change cart contents before confirming the order.

After confirmation, changes are not guaranteed because preparation may already have started.

Customers should contact restaurant support as soon as possible.

A requested change is completed only after the live ordering system or restaurant staff confirms it.

## Cancellation

A takeaway order may be cancelled only according to the refund and cancellation policy.

Cancellation is not guaranteed after preparation has started.

A cancellation or refund should be treated as approved only after confirmation from the live ordering system or restaurant staff.

## Missing, incorrect or damaged items

Customers should inspect the order within a reasonable time after collection.

Missing, incorrect or damaged items should be reported through the ordering service or restaurant support.

Customers may be asked to provide:

* The order reference
* The affected item
* A description of the issue
* Supporting photographs where appropriate

A replacement, refund or account credit is not automatic and requires review under the complaint and refund policies.

## Takeaway limitations

The restaurant cannot guarantee:

* Takeaway availability from every branch
* Immediate preparation
* An exact preparation or ready time
* That an order is ready before its status is confirmed
* Collection from a branch other than the branch selected at checkout
* Curbside collection unless it is offered by the live ordering system
* Indefinite storage of an uncollected order
* A refund, remake or account credit for late or missed collection
* A change or cancellation before it has been confirmed
