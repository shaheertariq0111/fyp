# General Frequently Asked Questions

## What services does the restaurant provide?

The restaurant currently supports delivery and customer takeaway orders.

Dine-in service, table reservations and catering are not currently supported through the ordering assistant.

## How can I place an order?

Orders can currently be placed through the restaurant's web ordering assistant or through a secure online menu link provided by the assistant.

WhatsApp ordering should not be treated as available unless it is explicitly enabled by the restaurant.

## Do I need to create an account?

A password-based customer account is not required to browse the menu or begin an order.

The ordering system may use a customer identifier to maintain the current cart, retrieve eligible order information and remember approved customer details. Customers may be required to confirm their contact or delivery information before checkout.

## Can I browse the menu without placing an order?

Yes. Customers may browse the available menu and ask about menu items before adding anything to their cart.

Current menu items, prices and availability must always come from the live ordering system.

## Can menu items be customized?

Menu items may only be customized using the sizes, crusts, toppings, removals, quantities and add-ons offered by the live ordering system.

The ordering assistant cannot guarantee a customization that is not available as a supported menu option.

## Can I change my cart?

Customers may add, remove or update cart items before the order is confirmed, subject to the options supported by the ordering system.

The customer should review the cart, quantities, customizations, fulfilment method and total before confirming the order.

## When is an order considered confirmed?

A cart or pending checkout is not a confirmed order.

An order is considered confirmed only when the ordering system explicitly confirms it and provides an order reference or order ID.

## Can I change an order after confirmation?

Changes after confirmation are not guaranteed because preparation may already have started.

The customer should contact restaurant support as soon as possible. The restaurant will determine whether the requested change can still be made.

## Are delivery and takeaway both available?

Yes. Customers may select delivery or takeaway when those fulfilment methods are available through the ordering system.

Delivery availability may depend on the selected branch, delivery address and current operational conditions.

## Can I schedule an order for later?

Scheduled ordering is not currently supported unless the ordering system explicitly presents a scheduling option.

The assistant must not promise that an order can be prepared or delivered at a future time when no scheduling option is available.

## Does the restaurant offer promotions or discount codes?

Only promotions, discounts and eligibility conditions shown by the live ordering system are valid.

The ordering assistant cannot create, extend, manually apply or guarantee a promotion that is not available through the system.

## Are preparation and delivery times guaranteed?

No. Any preparation or delivery time provided by the restaurant is an estimate and may change because of order volume, traffic, weather, item preparation requirements or operational conditions.

Detailed delivery guidance is provided through the approved delivery policy and branch information.

## Can the restaurant prepare items that are not on the menu?

No guarantee can be made for products, ingredients, sizes or customizations that are not available through the live menu.

Customers may ask for alternatives, but the assistant must recommend only menu items returned by the ordering system.

## Where can I get help?

Customers can request assistance through the ordering assistant.

Questions involving complaints, refunds, cancellations, allergies, payments or delivery issues must be handled according to the relevant approved restaurant policy.
