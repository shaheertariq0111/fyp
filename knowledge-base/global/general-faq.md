# General Frequently Asked Questions

## What services does the restaurant provide?

The restaurant currently supports delivery and takeaway orders.

Dine-in service, table reservations and catering are not currently supported through this ordering service.

## How can I place an order?

Orders can currently be placed through the restaurant's web ordering service or through a secure online menu link provided by the restaurant.

WhatsApp ordering is available only when the restaurant explicitly enables and presents that option.

## Do I need to create an account?

A password-based customer account is not required to browse the menu or begin an order.

The ordering system may use a customer or session identifier to:

* Maintain the current cart
* Retrieve order information that the customer is permitted to access
* Remember customer details that were previously provided and saved
* Continue an active ordering session

Customers may need to confirm their contact or delivery information before checkout.

## Can I browse the menu without placing an order?

Yes. Customers may browse the available menu and ask about menu items without adding anything to their cart.

Current menu items, prices, sizes, customizations and availability are provided by the live ordering system.

Menu information may change and should be checked again before order confirmation.

## Can menu items be customized?

Menu items may be customized only using the sizes, crusts, toppings, removals, quantities and add-ons offered by the live ordering system.

A customization that is not displayed as an available option cannot be guaranteed.

Removing an ingredient does not necessarily make an item suitable for an allergy or dietary requirement.

## Can I change my cart?

Customers may add, remove or update cart items before the order is confirmed, subject to the options supported by the ordering system.

Before confirmation, customers should review:

* Cart items
* Quantities
* Sizes and customizations
* Fulfilment method
* Delivery or collection details
* Final payable amount

## When is an order considered confirmed?

A cart, draft order or pending checkout is not a confirmed order.

An order is confirmed only when the live ordering system explicitly confirms it and provides an order reference or order ID.

A message stating that the customer wants to order does not by itself confirm an order.

## Can I change an order after confirmation?

Changes after confirmation are not guaranteed because preparation may already have started.

Customers should contact restaurant support as soon as possible.

A requested change is completed only when the live ordering system or restaurant staff confirms it.

## Are delivery and takeaway both available?

Customers may select delivery or takeaway when those fulfilment methods are presented by the live ordering system.

Delivery availability may depend on:

* The selected branch
* The delivery address
* Current operating hours
* Restaurant and driver capacity
* Temporary operational conditions

Takeaway availability may depend on the selected branch and current operating conditions.

## Can I schedule an order for later?

Scheduled ordering is not currently supported unless the live ordering system explicitly presents a scheduling option.

When no scheduling option is displayed, an order cannot be guaranteed for a future preparation, collection or delivery time.

## Does the restaurant offer promotions or discount codes?

Only promotions, discounts and eligibility conditions displayed by the live ordering system are valid.

A promotion may depend on:

* Eligible menu items
* Minimum order value
* Branch
* Fulfilment method
* Customer eligibility
* Validity period
* Other displayed conditions

A promotion cannot be created, extended, manually applied or guaranteed when it is not available through the ordering system.

## Are preparation and delivery times guaranteed?

No. Preparation, collection and delivery times are estimates.

Actual times may change because of:

* Order volume
* Item preparation requirements
* Traffic
* Weather
* Branch capacity
* Driver availability
* Temporary operational conditions

Customers should rely on the latest order-status information provided by the live ordering system.

## Can the restaurant prepare items that are not on the menu?

Products, ingredients, sizes and customizations that are not available through the live menu cannot be guaranteed.

Customers may ask about alternatives, but only items and options currently offered by the live ordering system can be ordered.

## Where can I get help?

Customers may request assistance through the restaurant's ordering service or restaurant support.

Depending on the issue, support may help with:

* General restaurant questions
* Delivery or takeaway policies
* Payment questions
* Order-status requests
* Complaints
* Refund and cancellation requests
* Allergy and dietary questions
* Human support requests

Some requests require confirmation or review by restaurant staff.

## Service limitations

The restaurant cannot guarantee:

* Dine-in, reservations or catering through this service
* WhatsApp ordering unless it has been enabled
* An unsupported menu item or customization
* A scheduled order unless scheduling is displayed
* A promotion that is not available through the live ordering system
* An exact preparation, collection or delivery time
* A post-confirmation order change before it has been confirmed
